A.M. OAKLEY

A.M. Oakley wasn't raised on bedtime
stories - she created her own

A latchkey kid with her head int he clouds
she turned to imagination as a way to
escape a world that offered little softness.
Over time, those stories - once whispered
to no one - became bedtime rituals for her
own children, and eventually, full-fledged
worlds of their own

Her writing carries the heart of that
journey: tehnder, vivid, and fiercely human.
She writes for the quiet kids, the
dreamers, and anyone who's everhad to
build their own magic from scratch

These days, she lives in a dessert city that
dreams of rain, with her husband, her
children, a wolf-hearted dog, and a bold
little mockingbirdwho sings back when
called...
